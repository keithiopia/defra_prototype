Basic Payment Scheme

You must apply for this scheme before 16 May 2015.

Basic eligibility met

This business is eligible for this scheme.

Greening requirements not met

30% of the basic payment is dependant on meeting greening requirements. This business does not currently meet these requirements because:

  • less than 5% of it's non-organic arable land has been designated as EFAs
  • it doesn't meet the crop diversification rules

What you can do

  • add more EFAs to your land
  • use EFAs from your organic land
  • request an excemption from the crop-diversification rules
