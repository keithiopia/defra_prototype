Basic Payment Scheme

The Basic Payment Scheme pays farmers annually for the land that they manage or own.

Eligibility requirements

To be eligible for the full Basic Payment you must:

  • be an active farmer
  • have at least 5ha of entitled arable land or grassland
  • meet the cross Compliance rules
  • meet the greening requirements

Cross Compliance rules

To be eligible for the Basic Payment Scheme, farmers must follow the relevant Cross Compliance rules for their business. The rules are there to encourage responsible, environmentally aware farming.

Greening requirements

30% of the Basic Payment is conditional on you meeting the following requirements:

  • Your main crop accounts for less than 75% of your total arable area Yes
  • Your 2 main crops account for less than 95% of your total arable area Yes
  • At least 5% of your arable land is made of Ecological Focus Areas No
